Who Are We?

Welcome to Plant Life, a premium monthly plant subscription box focused on wellness and sustainability! We pair with local nurseries in our delivery areas to bring you the highest quality plants with a smaller eco-footprint.

We have plans that cater to every lifestyle: Bring nature indoors with our House Plant option, create a sustainable food source with our Edible Garden option, or support your local ecosystem with our Native Plant option!

Try plant life for yourself or order for a friend - risk free! We give you the power to change or cancel your plan at anytime.

Why Choose Plant Life?

Ease of Care

We do the research for you! Every Plant Life delivery includes a detailed information card, care instructions, and everything you need to maintain the plant's health as it adapts to a new environnement.

Flexibility

You're in control!
We don't believe in contracts. Feel free to change your plan or frequency whenever you like.
Unhappy with our service? You can cancel your plan at any time.

Sustainability

We care about the world we live in! We seek to reduce our company's environmental footprint, always using eco-friendly/ recycled packaging and sourcing our plants from growers local to your area.
